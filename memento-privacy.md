# Privacy Policy for Memento Mori

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2025

## 1. Data Collection & Usage
Memento Mori is a "Local-Only" extension.
* **We do not collect, transmit, or sell your data.**
* **No external servers:** All settings, statistics, goals, and personal inputs (such as birthdate) are stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's `storage.local` API.
* **No Analytics:** We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking scripts.

## 2. Permissions Justification
* **Storage:** Used to save your preferences and blocking schedules locally.
* **ActiveTab / Tabs:** Used strictly to detect if the current website matches your "Blocked List" so we can redirect you to the focus page.
* **Notifications:** Used to send local alerts for daily reminders and schedule updates.
* **Idle:** Used to detect when you are away from the computer to pause the screen time counter.

## 3. Data Removal
You are in full control. If you uninstall the extension, all data stored by Memento Mori is permanently deleted by the browser.

## 4. Contact
If you have questions, please open an issue on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/AmazingKeymaster/memento-mori/issues).
